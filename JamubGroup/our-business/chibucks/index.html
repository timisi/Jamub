Chibucks Mines

Jamub Group operates the largest coal mining to generate power as an alternative to gas since the supply of gas has been plagued with incessant disruptions.

Over 50 percent of the power needs of the cement plants are generated from coal. Our coal mining initiative is benefiting both the company and the Nigerian economy by reducing the need for foreign exchange and helping us to both protect existing jobs and create new ones.
