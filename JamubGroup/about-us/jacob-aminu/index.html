Prince Jacob Momoh

Group Chief Executive Officer

Prince Jacob Aminu Momoh, is a renowned Nigerian businessman who had always nursed the ambition of being an employer of labour and a solutionist accross all human endeavours. This necessitated the establishment of Jamub Group of companies from its pioneer subsidiaries, Jamub Global Services Limited. Today, Jamub Group can boast of a number of reputable subsidiaries, among them: Jamub Property Nigeria Limited, Jamub Construction Limited, John-Paul Pharmaceuticals Limited, Jamub Energy Limited, Urban Project Prospects Integrated Services Ltd (UPPISL), SchoolMe Investment Services Ltd, Stage by Stage Limited, Pundit Agro-Allied Products Limited etc.

Prince Momoh currently oversees the Group as the Group Managing Director/Chief Executive Officer, and has continued pursuing a growth agenda for the conglomerate managed on the basis of core values of commitment, responsibility, accountability, integrity, excellence and quality delivery. He has creditably helped the Group break through the most difficult times, standing and scaling through even economic downturns and setbacks experienced in the country. His passion in providing solutions has kept him sailing to success.

As a philanthropist with a large heart, he has over the years empowered many young people in various vocations and trades as well as supported many others who had academic dreams but were financially handicapped. He has continuously touched lives directly or indirectly through wife’s NGO, Experanza Momoh Foundation, where they are saddled with the responsibility of reaching out to the vulnerable in the society by providing them with basic things of life to help reduce the rate of hooliganism and crime in the society and since inception has touched 100,000 Nigerians and counting.

Prince Momoh is a recipient of several awards for his exceptional managerial and leadership competence as well as for his contribution towards national development. Notable among these is the World Habitat Humanitarian and Philanthropic Award for Corporate Enterprise and Leadership by the World Habitat Foundation received in 2017. He is also a fellow of the Chartered Institute of Project Managers of Nigeria, Institute of Business Diplomacy and Financial Management as well as the Institute of Corporate Administration, he holds a BSc (Hon) in criminology and security studies from National Open University and he is PMP certified.
